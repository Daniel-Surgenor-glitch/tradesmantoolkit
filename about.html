About Tradesman Toolkit

Welcome to the Tradesman Toolkit, a high-quality suite of free web resources designed to streamline daily business processes for the worldwide tradesmen community. We cover estimates, billing invoices, quotes, and structural material calculations for plasterers, painters, decorators, carpenters, builders, plumbers, and small masonry contractors.

Our Core Principles

100% Privacy Sandbox All details entered inside invoices, client logs, or plaster equations are stored solely within your browser's locally sandboxed storage. We have no external database databases, meaning zero trade statistics are ever tracked or recorded by us.
Absolutely Free & Ad-Supported Our goal is to remain a permanent free tool. To fund infrastructure maintenance and ongoing calculators formulas updates, we allocate clear banner slots for Google AdSense networks. Ads maintain this system forever.

Full Compatibility with Static Hosting

Because this entire application contains absolutely 0 backend processing (no PHP, Node.js servers, SQL server clusters, or background daemons), it is 100% compatible with GitHub Pages static hosting rules, and can even be run entirely offline directly on your local laptop on-site.
